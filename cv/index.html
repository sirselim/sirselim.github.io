Miles C. Benton

Bioinformatics PhD and data scientist

miles.benton84@gmail.com | personal website | sirselim | @miles_benton

Currently

I am PostDoc Research Fellow at QUT working on the creation of methods to deal with, and analyse, large genomic data sets. These methods include the incorporation of multiple layers of both phenotypic and genomic data. I work on a range of complex human disorders, including; obesity and type-2 diabetes, multiple sclerosis, glaucoma, migraine, ovarian cancer and asthma.
